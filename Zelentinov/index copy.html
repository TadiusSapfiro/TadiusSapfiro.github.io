Hello, I’m Volodymyr,
Frontend developer and business process automation engineer from Estonia– I create digital products with knowledge and passion.

I started my way as a frontend developer in October 2018 being studied at the Donetsk State Medical University. Absolutely unrelated areas, but programming attracted me much more, and therefore I decided to connect my life with web. My main passions its optimization and accessibility. I consider it our duty to make products accessible to everyone, including people with weak machines or people with special needs.

My strategic thinking allows me to assess a product's holistic scope and drive its evolution by integrating new business ideas, innovative solutions, and added value that align with both user needs and business objectives. Beyond crafting user flows, information architecture, and wireframes, I consistently develop tailor-made interactive prototypes to test concepts and emphasize a user-centered approach.

I leverage the latest techniques in web development, using an agile workflow built on carefully chosen tools and strategies. These include object-oriented patterns, build scripts, preprocessors, minification, source code management, testing, and deployment to ensure efficiency and high-quality results.
